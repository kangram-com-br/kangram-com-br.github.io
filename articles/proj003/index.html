Live Lab

30. May 2016

[in progress] open source, browser-based toolkit for networked performance, that uses a peer-to-peer mesh network to share video, audio, and data streams between individuals and venues.

Developed by Jesse Ricke and Olivia Jack. Supported by CultureHub Art & Technology Center, La MaMa Experimental Theater Club, and Seoul Institute of the Arts. Project selected for Interactivos? 16: Possible Worlds. Creative and Collaborative Uses of Digital Technology at MediaLab-Prado in Madrid, June 2016.
